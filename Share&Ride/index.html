about Share&Ride

Share & Ride is your “one-stop-shop” for transportation from and to Independent University. Whether it’s biking, carpooling, CNG pooling, or Rickshaw pooling, be ensured that we will get you moving in the right direction. Our staff is dedicated to reducing the number of single occupant vehicles on while going to or coming back from Independent University, making it easier for commuters to get to their desired destination on time.

How Share & Ride works ?

Website:

By browsing into the website, you will be able to download the app, Share & Ride on your android smartphone. The website provides various details about the app. You will have to register through the website in order to become a verified user of the application, Share & Ride. In order to contact the staff of Share & Ride, just click on the Contact page for all the information and details.

App:

At first, the users will have to register themselves by providing the via IUB email ID, IUB ID number, uploading a photograph and providing a picture of the IUB ID card. Check the various ride posts. If you have found the perfect ride just send a request for the ride to the desired destination.Once the user accepts your request, both of you can discuss further about the ride through the chat box of the app.

Safety:

Our goal is to provide a safe and affordable trip to and from the Independent University. In order to maintain safety, the users will have to provide the following for verification. This in turn enables to build trust within our community and gives the users a greater sense of safety when travelling on Share & Ride. A verified IUB email id, The IUB details, photographs, contacts details etc.

How do you verify the users in the app?

In order to book a ride both person (one who posted the ride and another looking for a ride) need to verify by registering via NSU email ID, NSU ID number, uploading a photograph and providing a picture of the IUB ID card. Also the user needs to provide their phone number which will only be shown to each other after you book a ride.

How is the ride price set?

In Share & Ride, each user sets their own ride price. If the user is riding via private car, the owner of the private car or motorbike can set his/ her desired amount based on the length of the trip, fuel or CNG cost etc. In case of rickshaw or CNG, once the rickshaw puller or CNG driver sets the price, the users can calculate the amount each have to pay via Share & Ride app calculator.

How to recognize the user with whom I will share my ride?

Each ride includes the name of the user, his or her photograph, contact details, and the type of vehicle he or she will ride on. We highly recommend contacting the user before the ride to make sure you know exactly where and when the ride begins. There is a chat box where you can chat with the user and also call the user if needed.

If something comes up, can I cancel a ride?

At any point until the ride time you may cancel your ride just by clicking “Cancel” in the ride details page. That said, it is recommended that, if you must cancel the ride, please do so as far in advance as you can so the user can accept other ride requests.

How do I cancel or edit an existing ride?

You can do so within the ride details. For a recurring ride, you can choose to edit a particular ride only or apply the change to all such rides.
